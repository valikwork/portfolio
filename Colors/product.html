Universal enamel

Fast-drying alkyd enamel strengthened with acrylates (colors correspond with European classificator RAL). For exterior and interior use. Makes a strong gloss coat resistant to mechanic and weather exposure. Does not fade out in the sunlight and remains glossy.

Used for painting metal, concrete, plasterboard, timber, glass, paper, and other surfaces. Perfect for stencil-plates.

Safety data sheet

For better gloss and protection apply colorless Lacquer Universal BIODUR as a finishing coat 30 min after applying the last coat of the enamel. For a matt effect use Lacquer Universal Matt BIODUR.

- Spreading capacity (depending on coat thickness) is — 1.5-2.0 м2.

- Drying time 7-10 min at 25°C.
